Mission & Vision


Vision

Our vision at Prospect college is to impact quality knowledge and build students to be outstanding both academically and morally.

Mission

Our mission at Prospect College:

  • Device the best teaching method to make learning easy and fun
  • Guide our students to unleash their full potentials
  • Adopt the best learning standard to make our students compete with their mates both within and outside the country
  • Create an avenue for skill acquisition
  • Build our students to be of good conducts with their mates and in the society.
  • Create a conducive environment for learning
